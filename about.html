About Coreli

Empowering self-discovery through comprehensive personality insights and psychological assessments

Our Mission

At Coreli, we believe that understanding your personality is the first step toward personal growth and meaningful relationships. Our comprehensive personality assessment system is designed to provide deep insights into your unique traits, preferences, and behavioral patterns.

We've developed a sophisticated 16-type personality system that goes beyond traditional assessments to capture the nuances of human behavior. Our approach considers four key dimensions: Justice vs Victory, Stability vs Chaos, Radiator vs Absorber, and Consistency vs Burst.
